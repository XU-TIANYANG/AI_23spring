Artificial Intelligence Introductions is an independent course for junior students major in computer science. AI - perceiving, synthesizing, and inferring information - demonstrated by machines, as opposed to intelligence displayed by non-human animals and humans. Example tasks in which this is done include speech recognition, computer vision, translation between (natural) languages, as well as other mappings of inputs, also playing an important role in smart cities design and construction. The basic objective of this course is to enable students to understand the background and advances of artificial intelligence, understand the classical knowledge and its application, and lay the foundation for further learning in the next stage.
